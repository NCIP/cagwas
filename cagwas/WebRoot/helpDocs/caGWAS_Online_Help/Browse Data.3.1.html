Browsing caGWAS Data
You can search the caGWAS database for specific genes or regions of interest. You can browse by:
•
Association Finding, to identify regions of chromosomes associated with cancer
•
Population Frequency, to determine the SNP frequency by population type
•
Subjects Data, to retrieve data specific to patients and controls (available to registered users only)
