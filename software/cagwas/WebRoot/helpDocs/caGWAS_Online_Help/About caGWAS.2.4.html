Providing Feedback
Please help us improve the caGWAS data portal by providing your feedback.
To provide feedback to the caGWAS team
1.
Navigate to About caGWAS > Contact Us > Feedback. The Feedback page appears.
2.
Select caGWAS features from either of the two lists, as you prefer.
3.
In the text box, enter any other feedback you have.
4.
Click Submit.
To clear your feedback and start over, click Reset.
The NCI does not track any information through this feedback form that can identify you. However, if you would like to be contacted, please provide your contact information in the text box.
