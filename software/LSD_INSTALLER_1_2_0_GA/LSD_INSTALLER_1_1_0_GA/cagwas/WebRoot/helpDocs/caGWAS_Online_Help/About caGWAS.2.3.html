Using caGWAS Online Help
Online help explains how to use all of the features of the caGWAS data portal.
To access online help in the caGWAS data portal, use either of the following methods:
•
Click How to use the caGWAS data portal in the box in the right-hand column of the home page to access all help pages.
•
Click on any page to get help for the information or tasks on that page.
See the following tips for locating topics of interest, navigating the help, and printing a topic.
To locate topics of interest
•
To highlight your current topic in the table of contents, click .
•
To view the help index, click the Index tab at the top of the table of contents pane. Click any index term to show the related page.
•
To do a word search, click the Search tab at the top of the table of contents pane. Enter the query text in the search text box and then click Go!.
•
To create a list of favorite topics, navigate to the topic, click the Favorites tab at the top of the table of contents pane, and then click Add. The topic appears in the Favorites tab where you can easily retrieve it later.
•
For definitions of terms used in the caGWAS data portal, navigate to About caGWAS Data > Glossary in the table of contents, or hover your mouse over an italicized term in online help.
To use navigation tools
•
The breadcrumb trail of topics at the top of the page, for example, Browsing caGWAS Data > Searching SNP Subject Data, shows the path you took to get to your current page. Click a breadcrumb link to go back to a previous page you’ve viewed.
•
Follow blue hypertext links or the Related Topics buttons on the help page to open closely related topics. You can also click at the top right of the help page to see the related topics.
•
To expand or narrow the width of the table of contents or topic pane, click and drag the divider between them.
To print a help topic
•
Click at the top right of the help page to print your current topic.
