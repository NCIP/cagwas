Requesting Data for More than 500 Records
If your report exceeds 500 records, you will not receive your data immediately but rather once a report is prepared for you. You will receive an email letting you know that this report is available for downloading at an FTP site. You will have five days to download your report.
Enter your email address in the space provided so that we can let you know when your report is ready.
