Logging In to caGWAS
If you are a registered user, logging in to the caGWAS data portal allows you to search password-protected subject data and genotype data, as well as publicly available association finding and population frequency data.
To log in to caGWAS
1.
Enter your Username and Password.
If you forgot your password, go to https://password.nci.nih.gov to reset it. Click Forgotten Password for tips on choosing a new password.
2.
Click Submit.
If you are not a registered user, you can apply for an account
To apply for an account to access the caGWAS data portal
1.
At the top of any caGWAS page, click the Login link.
2.
Click the Register for Access button. A page of instructions for becoming a registered user appears.
